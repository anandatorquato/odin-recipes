Rice

Rice is the seed of the grass species Oryza sativa (Asian rice) or less commonly Oryza glaberrima (African rice).

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1.5 cup boiled water

Step by step process

  1. Bring to simmer on medium high.
  2. Turn down to low, cover with lid, cook 13 min
  3. Rest 10 min
