Banana Pancake

Make these foolproof dairy and egg-free banana pancakes. A guaranteed crowd pleaser with a fluffy, light texture, you'd never know they’re vegan

Ingredients:

  • 1 large ripe banana (around 150g)
  • 2 tbsp golden caster sugar
  • ¼ tsp fine salt
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil , plus extra for cooking
  • 120g self-raising flour
  • ½ tsp baking powder
  • 150ml oat, almond milk or soya milk
  • syrup , sliced banana and berries, to serve (optional)

Step by step process

  1. Mash the banana in a mixing bowl.
  2. Stir in the sugar, salt and oil.
  3. Add the flour and baking powder and mix thoroughly.
  4. Make a well in the centre and gradually whisk in the milk.
  5. Heat a little oil in a frying pan over a medium heat.
  6. Add 2 tbsp of the batter to make American-style pancakes.
  7. You will be able to make about 4-5 at a time.
  8. Fry on each side for 2-3 mins until golden.
  9. Serve with syrup, sliced banana and berries, if you like.
